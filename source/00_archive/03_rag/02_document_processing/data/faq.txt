FAQ

Q: Why do we create stable document IDs?
A: Stable IDs make repeated indexing predictable and allow later phases to update or delete documents consistently.

Q: Why is metadata important?
A: Metadata is how the system knows where a chunk came from and how it should be filtered or cited.

Q: Why not implement embeddings now?
A: This phase is only responsible for turning raw files into reliable chunks. Embeddings arrive in Phase 3.
